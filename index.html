Key Insights

  • The top 3 brands (USPA, Penti, CAMPUS) all peaked in Wk 3 (Mar 15–21), suggesting a common demand driver mid-month.
  • French Connection peaked earlier in Wk 2, while Keds and Vero Moda peaked in Wk 4 — different demand cycles across categories.

  • Not all brands peaked in the same week — this points to brand-specific campaigns or category demand rather than a single platform-wide event.
